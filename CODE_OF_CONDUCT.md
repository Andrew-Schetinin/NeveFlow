# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good intent, and keep discussions focused on the work.

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. 
All reports will be reviewed and responded to promptly and confidentially.
